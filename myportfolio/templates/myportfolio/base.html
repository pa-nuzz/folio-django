About

I'm a backend engineer who thinks in systems, not just features.

My work centers on building structured backend systems that solve real problems. I approach engineering with curiosity about how things work beneath the surface.

Recently, I've been exploring the intersection of backend development and artificial intelligence — working with LLMs, experimenting with ML tools, and learning data science fundamentals.

I'm constantly learning new AI tools, diving deep into system architecture, and thinking about how to build things that are both powerful and maintainable.
